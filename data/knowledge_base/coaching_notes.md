# Sales Rep Best Practices and Coaching Notes

## High-Performing Rep Characteristics

Research across our top 20% of reps shows the following consistent behaviors:

1. **Proactive Pipeline Management**: Top reps update deal stages daily, not weekly.
   They treat CRM hygiene as part of their job, not a chore.

2. **Multi-threading Accounts**: Best reps establish 3+ contacts per account at 
   different levels (economic buyer, technical buyer, champion). Single-threaded 
   deals close at 40% lower rates.

3. **Discovery First**: High performers spend 60% of early calls in discovery mode —
   understanding pain, urgency, and decision criteria — before pitching.

4. **Timely Follow-up**: Top reps follow up within 24 hours of every meeting.
   Every meeting should end with a clear next step scheduled.

## Common Red Flags in Rep Performance

- **Stale pipeline**: Deals sitting in the same stage for 30+ days without activity
- **Few new opportunities**: Not prospecting (pure farming behavior)
- **Low discovery scores**: Call recordings show too much talking, not enough listening  
- **Single deal dependency**: More than 30% of quota in one deal is a risk concentration issue
